## FAQs

#### When and where is the Punahou Carnival?

#### What are the main features of the app?
1. Wait Times
   - View estimates of wait times for various booths (with confidence levels and ranges)
   - Submit reports of wait times and comments!
2. Interactive Map
   - View the locations for booths, scrip exchange stands, rides, and games on campus
   - See your position on campus and surrounding activities
3. Carnival Information
   - View the schedule for carnival on Friday and Saturday
   - See parking, payment, safety, and supervision info for carnival
   - View info for carnival shows, activities, games, and food
4. Game Center
   - Geocache: Find hidden QR codes scattered around campus for points
   - Balloon Pop: Pop balloons before they fly away!
   - Memory Match: Find the matching pairs of carnival-themed icons in as few moves as possible
   - Carnival Unscramble: Unscramble carnival-themed words 
   - Toucan Fly: Flap the Toucan through the bamboo

#### How do I buy food and play games at the Punahou Carnival?
Only scrip (no cash) will be accepted at Punahou booths or games, and only credit and debit cards (no cash) may be used to purchase scrip from Scrip Booths. Students will be able to purchase scrip using cash at Dillingham Box Office.

#### Where can I find more information/updates?
See the [website for the Punahou Carnival](https://carnival.punahou.edu/), which has information on food, entertainment, attractions, shopping, and more! In addition, you may email __carnival@punahou.edu__ for any questions or feedback. You can also follow us on Instagram @Punahou_Carnival.
